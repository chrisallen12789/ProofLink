Refunds and cancellations

We want the customer and the business to have a clear understanding of what happens if work is canceled, rescheduled, or disputed after it has been scoped or booked.

Cancellations before work starts

  • Contact us as soon as possible if the work needs to be canceled or rescheduled.
  • If the job has not been scheduled or materials have not been committed, we can usually adjust without issue.
  • If labor, materials, travel, or reserved time have already been committed, some charges or deposits may be retained.

Deposits

  • Deposits may be required before certain work is booked or dispatched.
  • Unless otherwise stated in writing, deposits hold time and planning for the job and may be non-refundable once preparation begins.

Completed work and concerns

If there is a concern after the work is completed, contact us promptly with a clear description of the issue and any supporting photos or notes. We review concerns based on the approved scope, the work performed, and any documented site conditions.
