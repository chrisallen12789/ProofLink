Refunds and cancellations

Because items are made to order, refunds are limited.

Cancellations

  • If you need to cancel, contact us as soon as possible by email.
  • If production has not started, we will generally cancel without issue.
  • If production has started, we may not be able to refund costs already incurred.

Quality issues

If something is wrong with an item you received, contact us within 24 hours with a description and a photo. We will review and make it right when appropriate.

No returns

For food safety reasons, we do not accept returns of baked goods.
