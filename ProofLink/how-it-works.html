How it works

1) Build your request

Browse the Products page and add items to your cart.

2) Submit request

Go to Cart and tell us anything important: timing goals, ingredient questions, preferences.

3) Confirm by email

We confirm what is realistic for your pickup window and total cost before baking.

We are bake to order. That protects quality and prevents assumptions about what you meant. You stay in control; we handle the execution.

Allergens and cross contact?

This is a home kitchen that uses wheat, eggs, dairy, and nuts. Cross contact with allergens is possible.

Made in a home kitchen that has not been inspected by the Michigan Department of Agriculture and Rural Development.
