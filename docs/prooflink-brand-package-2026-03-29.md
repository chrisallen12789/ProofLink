# ProofLink Brand Package

## Why this exists
ProofLink needs one visual and verbal system that feels steady, premium, and easy to trust. The brand should tell owners that this is where the business becomes easier to read.

## Team Recommendation
Marketing:
Lead with clarity, control, and fewer dropped balls instead of generic software language.

Customer psychology:
Reduce anxiety. Show that the customer account, work, and money are visible in one place.

Design:
Use a linked-orbit visual language that suggests proof, motion, and connected records.

Frontend:
Favor lightweight SVG and CSS motion over heavy 3D libraries so the site stays fast on phones.

## Brand promise
ProofLink gives service businesses one calm operating system for the customer account, the work, and the money.

## Voice
Direct, steady, operational, credible.

Avoid:
Buzzwords, hypey AI language, feature dumping, and jargon that sounds enterprise-only.

Use:
Clear outcomes, operational language, and proof-oriented phrases like "see the account", "track the work", and "know what is still unpaid".

## Motion system
The signature motion is an orbital field:
- linked rings
- calm depth
- slow parallax
- subtle pulsing nodes

The feeling should be "business control in motion", not "gamer 3D".

## Placement guidance
Use the mark for:
- favicons
- app switchers
- avatars
- compact nav states

Use the lockup for:
- landing page
- launch decks
- one-pagers
- email headers

## Rule of thumb
If a screen feels louder than the actual business data, the brand is getting in the way.
