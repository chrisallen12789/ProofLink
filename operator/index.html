Welcome to ProofLink. This is where you run your entire business — orders, customers, products, schedule, and money in one place.

Here's what each section does

  • Dashboard — Your daily snapshot. Jobs in, money out, what needs attention.
  • Orders — Every request that comes in. Tap one to review and update it.
  • Customers — Who ordered, how much they've spent, your notes on them.
  • Products — Add or edit the things you sell. Turn items on or off.
  • Availability — Set which days and hours customers can book you.
  • Expenses — Log costs so you can see your real profit.
  • Guidance — Plain-language advice based on your actual data.
