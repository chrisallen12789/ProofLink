Privacy

We collect the information customers submit through request forms, contact forms, payment flows, and follow-up messages so the business can review the work, confirm the next step, and keep a usable record of the relationship.

What we collect

  • Name, email, phone number, service address, and request details you choose to send.
  • Photos, notes, and scheduling details attached to a request or active job.
  • Payment and billing details needed to record deposits, invoices, or completed payments.

How the information is used

  • To review the request and confirm pricing, timing, or scope.
  • To deliver the work, document what happened, and keep customer communication organized.
  • To send service updates, receipts, reminders, or review follow-up tied to real business activity.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell customer information.
  • We do not send unrelated marketing just because you sent one request.
  • We do not share job details beyond the vendors, payment processors, or service providers needed to operate the business.

Data retention

We keep customer and job records as long as reasonably necessary to operate the business, answer questions, handle payment issues, and maintain normal business history.

Questions

If you have a privacy question, use the contact page and include the best way to reach you.
