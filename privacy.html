Privacy

We collect the information you submit through our contact and order request forms. This typically includes your name, email address, and the message you send.

How we use your information

  • To reply to your message and coordinate your order
  • To confirm details such as pickup time, delivery request, and customizations
  • To keep basic records of inquiries and orders for business operations

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your information
  • We do not use your information for unrelated marketing without your permission

Data retention

We keep messages as long as reasonably necessary for operational and record keeping purposes.

Third party services

Form submissions are processed through our hosting and email services so we can receive and respond to requests. Those providers may store logs required to operate the service.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, use the contact page and we will respond by email.
